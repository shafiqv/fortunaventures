Who Am I

The person behind the search

Shafiq Vallani
LinkedIn ↗

Shafiq's path to buying a business started well before adolescence. His grandfather immigrated to Canada in the 1970s and built a blue-collar hardware and lumber business from scratch. Shafiq spent much of his childhood running around the store and yard, watching what ownership actually looked like up close: the grind of it, but also the pride that comes along with it — employees building careers and families and customers who kept coming back because they were known by name.

Since then, Shafiq has started, invested in, and advised dozens of businesses, gaining immense tuition-value across venture capital (TELUS Global Ventures, Redesign Health), buy-side M&A (RBC Ventures), and operations in manufacturing and in consulting with Carpedia International and BDC. He's built capital markets experience at Red Cloud Securities and League Assets, and founded Gryphon Sports Management and ISBY.

Outside of work, he stays active across cycling, tennis, and hockey.

How This Works

From first conversation to close

A straightforward, confidential process. Most conversations start well before any formal process — often just an exploratory coffee.

Introductory conversation

A no-obligation call to understand your business, your timeline, and what matters most to you in a transition — whether you're actively exploring a sale or simply curious.

Mutual NDA & information exchange

High-level financials and operating details shared under a signed NDA to assess fit against acquisition criteria.

Indication of interest

A non-binding, preliminary view on valuation and structure if there's a fit — fast, and without dragging you through a lengthy process on a deal that isn't real.

Letter of intent

Agreed terms, exclusivity, and a defined diligence timeline — with financing structured well ahead of time, so it isn't a question mark late in the process.

Diligence & close

Focused, respectful diligence built around your team's bandwidth, followed by a transition plan you help shape.
